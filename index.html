my-journey.txt

I'm a 16-year-old from Pakistan. My journey started back in 2019 when I wanted to become a gaming YouTuber. My first video got around 70 views, 3 likes, and 12 dislikes. That was the moment I realized content creation was an actual structural skill that takes genuine precision.

Over the next few months, I got obsessed with video editing. Since I didn't even have a PC at the time, everything was edited on my phone using KineMaster. Eventually, my videos were getting 1–2k views each, and I built a community of about 11,000 subscribers.

When COVID came around, I got my first low-end laptop. It could barely run basic files, but I managed to get DaVinci Resolve working. Around that time, I started taking real media editing commissions through Discord groups to monetize my craft.

The turning point arrived in 2024 when a peer introduced me to HiddenDevs, a Discord ecosystem matching structural engineers and developers. Since I already had experience with Blender, building maps and custom assets felt native.

My very first 3D commission was a low-poly simulator environment asset bundle. It took two days to polish and brought in 700 Robux after marketplace tax. From there, I scaled operations, eventually teaming up with Amin to co-found GoUp.gg.
